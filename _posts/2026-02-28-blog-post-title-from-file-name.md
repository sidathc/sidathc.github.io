## Day 2: Narrowing it down to Australia

Seeing some trends from the Contrails dataset was pretty exciting! I wanted to get another look at my first small dataset by narrowing the week's worth of data to Australia.

I did this by creating a bounding box based on the coordinates of the Australian continent and generated the same plots from day 1 with the filtered data:

Although it is very early to tell if these results mean much, it is still interesting to see the differences between the day 2 and day 1 plots. 

Here, the most visible difference can be seen in the second plot. In both plots, the highest likelihood for contrail formation is on Wednesdays, but in the Australia subset the likelihood is even higher on Wednesdays and Thursdays.
