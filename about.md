## About This Blog
Most of the research on contrail formation has focused on Europe and North America. 

Growing up in Australia, I saw first-hand the reliance on long-haul air travel due to our geographic isolation from the rest of the world.

**Contrails Australia** is my personal blog to explore how contrail formation works in Australian skies. My goal is to use existing data to identify where "hotspots" for contrail formation exist, and identify some solutions of how we can avoid creating them.
